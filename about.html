Our Journey & Purpose

Behind Raghukul Aryawart

A Decade of Grassroots Change

Raghukul Aryawart is a decade-old NGO working directly at the grassroots level to improve social welfare, conserve our environment, and uplift underserved communities. We are run by a group of highly experienced professionals alongside energetic youth who are passionate about driving actionable changes.

Our operational model is entirely unique: **we do not accept any external funding, donations, or sponsorships.** We believe that true independence leads to authentic, uncompromised social service. To achieve this self-sustainability, we established Swadesi Hathkargha, our social-enterprise arm that employs local handloom artisans and weavers to fund our community projects.

Our Vision

To nurture a plastic-free, zero-waste, and deeply compassionate world where economic growth is in harmony with environmental conservation, and local heritage is preserved for generations.

"Self-sustainability is the ultimate freedom. By funding our community works through handloom products, we create a circular system of dignity, employment, and service."

Our Mission

To replace plastic habits with sustainable alternatives, deliver free quality education to underprivileged children, provide training to women, and support heritage handloom weavers.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

1

Independent Self-Sustainability

By generating our own income through Swadesi Hathkargha, we remain independent, transparent, and fully focused on pure public interest without any corporate or external influence.

2

Empowering Through Dignity

We do not hand out charity. We believe in providing skills, employment, and education so that women, weavers, and children can lead self-reliant lives with dignity.
