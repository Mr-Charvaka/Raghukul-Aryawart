Raghukul Aryawart

Raghukul Aryawart is a decade old NGO (Non-Government Organization) working for the various aspects of social life and wellbeing of society. It is being run by experienced as well as young enthusiasts, it works on unique model of self-sustainability that we don’t take any kind of funding and sponsorship from outside.

Initially funded by its members later on when it felt that it is very difficult to fuel-up big projects by self-funding, Swadesi Hathkargha, its financial arm has been created. Which work for welfare of handloom weavers, we take a very small profit margin to run other projects. RA works on the following areas of social concerns like..

Sustainable Living, Empowered Communities

1

Empowering Women Through Livelihoods

We create income-generating opportunities for women in rural and urban areas through skill development and employment in eco-friendly product manufacturing.

2

Educating the Underprivileged

We offer free education to children from below poverty line families and bring learning resources to remote regions where formal education is limited.

3

Remote location education

We bridge the education gap in remote and underserved areas by providing access to quality learning resources. Through mobile classrooms, trained volunteers, and digital tools, we empower children who lack formal schooling. Our mission is to ensure that no child is left behind, regardless of geography or background.
