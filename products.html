Swadesi Hathkargha Financial Arm

Eco-Friendly Products With Purpose

Funding Social Change, One Thread at a Time

Swadesi Hathkargha is the social financial arm of Raghukul Aryawart. We support local heritage handloom weavers and empower rural women by manufacturing organic, high-quality, and multi-purpose cloth bags (Jholas).

When you purchase our handloom bags, you are not just buying an eco-friendly product—**100% of the profits go directly into funding our NGO projects** such as BPL education classrooms, women hygiene drives, and road safety campaigns.
